= Work/life balance

Work/life balance is essential to successful software delivery. That's because the work is predominantly a thought process.

The brain needs time to process and incubate ideas. It's not a mechanical process. It's not a factory or production line. It's barely a process at all.

But good work/life balance starts at work. If I get tired at work, I need longer rest periods, which means I have less time to truly switch off and spend with my family and friends, or doing my hobbies.
